## 1.2 Scope

The proposed system is a web-based e-commerce platform that leverages generative artificial intelligence to automate the creation of product listings. Its primary objective is to support users—especially individuals without experience in online sales—by simplifying the process of publishing products. By allowing users to upload a single product image, the system will automatically generate the necessary information (title, description, tags, category), thus eliminating the need for manual data entry or tedious manual upload of hundreds of product's descriptions .

### Core Features

1. **User Management**: The system will include functionality for user registration, authentication, and profile management.
2. **AI-Based Product Publishing**:
   - Uploading a product image.
   - Automatic generation of title, description, tags, and categories using AI.
3. **Public Storefront**:
   - Display of all products in a browsable interface.
   - Product search and filtering by category or keyword.
     
### Benefits

- Reduces the technical barriers to entry for selling online.
- Saves time and cognitive effort by automating content generation.
- Ensures consistency and quality in product listings.
- Enables users to publish products with a single image.

### Limitations (Out of Scope for MVP)

- No integration with payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
- No shipping or logistics features.
- No order tracking or inventory management.
- No real-time chat or messaging between users.
- No mobile app (initial version is web-only and responsive).

This initial version will serve as a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) focused on validating the usefulness and usability of the core AI-powered publishing feature.

### 1.3 Target Group

The application is designed to serve two primary user groups:

1. **Non-technical individuals and first-time sellers**  
   Users who wish to sell products online but lack the technical expertise, time, or marketing skills to create appealing listings. This includes:
   - Small entrepreneurs and artisans
   - Informal sellers transitioning to digital platforms
   - Users unfamiliar with pricing strategies or product categorization

2. **High-volume sellers looking to optimize their workflow**  
   Users who manage a large number of products and need to automate repetitive tasks like writing descriptions, generating tags, and setting categories or prices. This includes:
   - Sellers with extensive inventories
   - Dropshippers and marketplace resellers
   - E-commerce managers seeking productivity tools

The system eliminates manual effort by automatically generating product titles, descriptions, tags, categories, and price suggestions from uploaded images. It enables both groups to publish products quickly and professionally, reducing time-to-market and improving listing quality with minimal input.
